Spanish Tortilla

Description

Delicious meal made of potatoes, onions and eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 4 eggs
  • oil

Steps

  1. Put some oil on a pan
  2. Before it's already hot, put the potatoes cut in little pieces
  3. After 3 minutes, put the onion cut in very thin slices
  4. After 15 minutes, break the eggs and shake them in a bowl
  5. Add salt and the potatoes and onions without the oil
  6. Shake everything inside the bowl
  7. Now add everything to the pan with hot oil
  8. After 5 minutes, turn the tortilla around and cook the other side for 3 minutes
